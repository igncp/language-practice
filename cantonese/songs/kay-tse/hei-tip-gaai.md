## 1/5

```

別再看塵封的囍帖
```

- 別再看：Don't look again
- 塵封的：Dusty
- 囍帖：Name of a street. Also, wedding invitation (for another person wedding)
- 搬家：To move place
- 築得起：Affordable
- 接受：To accept
- 都有日：All day
- 倒下：Fall down
- 一種：A sort of
- 安穩：Safety
- 快樂：Happyness
- 永遠：Forever
- 也不差：Not bad，also no difference
