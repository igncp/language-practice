## Meaning

- 心得: Experience, as in to experience an event
- 祝: To wish for someone
- 解決: To settle, to resolve
- 緊張: Nervous
- 流利: Fluent
- 移民: Immigrant
- 討論區: Forum
- 護照: Passport
- 聯盟 : Alliance
- 可恥 : Shameful
